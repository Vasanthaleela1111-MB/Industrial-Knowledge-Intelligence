About The Project

Industrial organizations manage thousands of engineering drawings, maintenance work orders, inspection reports, SOPs, OEM manuals, quality documents and regulatory records.

Most of these documents are distributed across multiple disconnected systems, making information retrieval slow and decision making difficult.

IndustrialMind AI unifies these documents into a centralized knowledge platform using Document Intelligence, Knowledge Graphs and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG).

The platform enables engineers to ask questions in natural language, retrieve accurate evidence and generate maintenance, quality and compliance insights.
